ABOUT ME

Hey, everyone! I'm Lívia, a 25 years old Journalist graduated from Faculdade Cásper Líbero. I'm a pop culture reporter and movie critic. I've covered famous entertainment events such as CCXP and BGS and I've interviewed singers and casts from the most famous cinema and streaming productions! I worked as a English Features Editor at a prestigious global magazine and even as a producer and host of a TV show! I also created a webseries about science fiction and future called Projetando o Futuro and you can follow me on social media to get into the life of a journalist of culture pop and a vintage lover! ♡
